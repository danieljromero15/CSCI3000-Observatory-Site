Crab Nebula

The Crab Nebula is a supernova remnant and pulsar wind nebula in the constellation of Taurus.

Orion Nebula

The Orion Nebula is a diffuse nebula situated in the Milky Way, being south of Orion's Belt in the constellation of Orion, and is known as the middle "star" in the "sword" of Orion.

Sombrero Galexy

The Sombrero Galaxy is a peculiar galaxy of unclear classification in the constellation borders of Virgo and Corvus, being about 9.55 megaparsecs from the Milky Way galaxy.

Horsehead Nebula

The Horsehead Nebula is a small dark nebula in the constellation Orion.

Cigar Galaxy

M82 Galaxy Messier 82 is a starburst galaxy approximately 12 million light-years away in the constellation Ursa Major.

Helix Nebula

The Helix Nebula is a planetary nebula located in the constellation Aquarius discovered by Karl Ludwig Harding.

MeerKAT Galactic Center

Radio emission from numerous phenomena, including outbursting stars, stellar nurseries, and the chaotic region around the 4 million solar mass supermassive black hole that lurks in the centre of our Galaxy, 25,000 light-years from Earth.
