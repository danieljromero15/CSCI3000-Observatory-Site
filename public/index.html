North Georgia Astronomical Observatory

The North Georgia Astronomical Observatory exists to promote astronomy education to
University of North Georgia students as well as the public. This state-of-the-art
facility is located only four miles from the UNG Dahlonega Campus.

3000 Dawsonville Hwy, Dahlonega, GA 30533

Meet our Telescopes!

0.6 Meter Aperture

Our 0.6 Meter Aperture Telescope is mounted on a 23.5 degree
angle allowing us to use the Equatorial Coordinate System for
tracking stars!

0.7 Meter Aperture

Our 0.7 Meter Aperture Telescope is mounted horizontally
allowing us to use the Horizontal Coordinate System when
tracking stars.
