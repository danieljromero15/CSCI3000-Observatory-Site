Exploring Space with NGAO!

North Georgia Astronomical Observatory

The North Georgia Astronomical Observatory exists to promote astronomy education to University of North Georgia students, as well as the public. This state-of-the-art facility is located only four miles from the UNG Dahlonega Campus.
